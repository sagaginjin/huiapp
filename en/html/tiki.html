Inspired by nature - crafted through generations

It is beautifully fragrant showing spiced cherry, game, dark mushroom,
leading to a silky-smooth palate. This wine is immediately appealing.

We are an indigenous New Zealand wine producer committed to
sustainable winegrowing with an uncompromising commitment to
quality. We care for our vines under the guiding Maori principles
of Kaitiakitanga: guardianship, protection and preservation of
the earth. Our family has been living and farming in New Zealand
(Aotearoa) for hundreds of years. We carefully select parcels
of fruit from our vineyards to make the very best wine possible.
Our winemaker aims for purity and freshness and a food friendly style.
