Annies - food you trust

Annies have a passion for health and wellness and believe the best
nutrition comes straight from nature because nature knows best. Our
promise is to only ever make food you trust. This means we only use
wholesome and honest ingredients. We make it real, so you can feel
good about what you are eating. Perfect for children, busy families,
sportspeople and the health conscious. Made from 100% natural fruit
which means, no concentrates (so more fibre stays in the fruit), no
added sugar, no sulphites, no gluten, no dairy, no preservatives, no
tree nuts or peanuts, no numbers, no nasties!

Annies is 100% owned by Kono NZ. Kono is a vertically integrated,
family owned Māori food and beverage producer – an artisan producer,
and exporter of award-winning wine, cider, seafood, fruit and natural
fruit bars. Māori are the indigenous people of Aotearoa New Zealand.
As a Māori company, we have a strong spiritual connection to the land,
to our culture and a tradition of kaitiakitanga, guardianship of our
natural resources. Kono means a traditional Maori food basket made of
woven flax, and the products we offer to the world combine to make a
basket of food.
