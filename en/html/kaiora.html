Every heavenly drop of Kai Ora's honey is lovingly produced
& harvested by third generation New Zealand Maori Beekeepers.

Kai Ora Honey is a 100% Maori owned family business with third
generation beekeeper’s rich with experience, cultural history and
knowledge. We have been raised to live well through traditional
plants and food grown wild to support our health and wellbeing.
Kai Ora was gifted to us from our ancestors which translates to
Food for Health Life and Wellbeing.

Kai Ora Honey specialises in producing 100% Authentic Manuka Honey
which is traceable right back to every hive site location. Every
heavenly drop is harvested in New Zealand, independently tested,
quality stamped and certified providing confidence for you and
your family.
