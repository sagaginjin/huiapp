Mana kai honey is a new zealand boutique beekeeping business
specialising in producing prestigious pure manuka honey,
carefully harvested from the honeycomb by our own beekeepers.

We are a Maori owned and operated business, located in the Far North
of New Zealand. Our story begins with our passion for bees and beekeeping.

We are commercial beekeepers operating 2000 beehives for honey production,
pollination and Queen rearing. Mana Kai Honey operates a honey extraction
facility servicing over 40 beekeepers and their combined 15,000 beehives.
Our honey house is listed as a registered exporter, operated under an RMP
which is administered by the New Zealand Ministry of Primary Industries
to produce Export Quality Honey.

Our Fernmark and Unique Manuka Factor (UMF) licences give you the
assurance that our Manuka Honey is of the finest purity and quality,
direct from the beekeeper.
