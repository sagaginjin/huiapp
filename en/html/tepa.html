Our te Pa range is for when you want to make an impact for family and friends.

te Pa has been home for 800 years - it is the site of some of
the earliest Maori landings to New Zealand. Haysley MacDonald
planted vines on his family’s land on the Wairau Bar in 2003
with a vision of creating a premium range of wines to connect
the taste of his home, te Pa, with wine lovers all around the
world. With winemaker Sam Bennett guiding the wines, te Pa now
produces exceptional Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Gris, Chardonnay,
Pinot Noir Rosé, and Pinot Noir, under the te Pa banner and
also two high quality, value ranges; Pa Road and Koha. Today,
te Pa is an international brand - but the taste of home is
still unmistakable.
