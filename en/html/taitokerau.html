We are a 100% Māori owned and operated beekeeping company located
in the Far North of Aotearoa (New Zealand). We currently run 5000 hives
and operate our own extraction and processing facilities in order to provide
pure, unadulterated honey to world-wide consumers that is transparent
from land to brand. This allows us to enhance sustainable natural and
economic growth for our people and our community. The concept of
sustainability is built into the foundation of our company, and is reflected
in our everyday practices, interactions and long-term goals. We take great
pride in maintaining healthy, happy bees!

As landowners, beekeepers, extractors, packers
and exporters we personally guarantee traceability
right from the land to your hand!
