TAHA belongs to Aotearoa-New Zealand – the land of the long white cloud.
Taha’s ingredients work side-by-side to bring to you a refreshing sparkling tonic from the edge of the World.

Taha Beverages is a niche beverage business that
was formed in 2012.

Taha Beverages produces a non-alcoholic quencher
made from, sustainably hand-picked Kawakawa with
Manuka honey & natural Ginger flavouring along
with pure New Zealand water.

Taha is derived from a fusion of traditional Asian
and New Zealand Maori herbal knowledge. The magic

ingredient in Taha is Kawakawa. The Kawakawa is
picked wild from forest on family land at Waitomo
Caves and the Bay of Islands. It is then sent to
our brewer Steam Brewing in Auckland where the
other ingredients are added and Taha is made.

TAHA translated from Maori has a number of
meanings including; to belong, side and edge.
We have infused these meanings into our beverage
